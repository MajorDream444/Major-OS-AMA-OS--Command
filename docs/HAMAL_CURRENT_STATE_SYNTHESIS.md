
Mission Control owns:
- prioritization

- canonization decisions
- cross-repo routing

Mission Control does not mean:
- every idea becomes a build

# 6. Graphify + Obsidian Shared Brain Role

Graphify and Obsidian form the durable shared brain layer.

Graphify maps relationships across files, repos, agents, workflows, artifacts, and source-of-truth boundaries. It is context infrastructure, not a product feature or external integration.

Obsidian preserves durable human-readable memory: decisions, synthesis notes, workspace maps, client-safe summaries, and cross-system explanations.

NotebookLM can support source-grounded review, explainers, and briefings, but it should point back to canonical markdown and source files.
